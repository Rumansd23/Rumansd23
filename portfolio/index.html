01 THE PERAK STORY

The Jawa Perak is a stunning bobber-style motorcycle that exudes vintage charm with a modern twist. Manufactured by Jawa Motorcycles, it is powered by a 334cc single-cylinder, liquid-cooled engine that delivers smooth performance with 30.64 PS of power and 32.74 Nm of torque. Its minimalist design, highlighted by a floating single seat, matte black paint, and retro-themed aesthetics, appeals to riders seeking a distinctive and timeless look. The Perak also features dual-channel ABS, telescopic front forks, and a mono-shock rear suspension for enhanced safety and comfort. With its perfect blend of classic elegance and contemporary engineering, the Jawa Perak stands out as a dream machine for motorcycle enthusiasts who crave individuality and sophistication on the road.

The motorcycle features a double cradle tubular frame, designed for stability and nimble handling. It is equipped with telescopic forks at the front and a monoshock suspension at the rear, which provides a comfortable ride. The Perak rolls on 18-inch front and 17-inch rear spoked wheels, fitted with 100/90 front and 140/70 rear tubed tyres. For safety, it boasts disc brakes on both ends, supported by dual-channel ABS.

Its design is accentuated by an elongated wheelbase of 1485 mm, a low seat height of 750 mm, and a ground clearance of 145 mm, making it both accessible and visually balanced. With a fuel tank capacity of 14 liters, it offers decent range for long rides. The Jawa Perak is priced competitively, targeting enthusiasts who appreciate its vintage charm blended with modern functionality. Its bold statement in the Indian bobber segment continues to captivate riders looking for a blend of heritage and individuality.
